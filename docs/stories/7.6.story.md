Checklist final pré-launch: verificação de produção (deployment, monitoring, backups), setup de suporte (docs, email, SLA), preparação operacional (accounts, pagamento, alertas), marketing/announcement, e decisão final go/no-go com critérios claros.

## ⚠️ Riscos Documentados

| Risco | Impacto | Mitigação |
|-------|---------|-----------|
| Last-minute blocker discovered | Launch delayed | Final QA gate 48h before, with go-nogo criteria |
| Production incident on day-1 | User dissatisfaction | Team on-call + incident response plan ready |
| Load spike causes outage | Bad press | Load test 2x expected peak, auto-scaling configured |
| Data loss due to backup failure | Catastrophic | Test restore 1 week before launch |
| Support overwhelmed | Bad experience | Pre-drafted responses + escalation plan |
| Marketing launches early (leak) | Competitive risk | Embargo coordination, scheduled posts reviewed |
| Legal issues (privacy, ToS) | Litigation risk | Legal review at least 2 weeks before |
