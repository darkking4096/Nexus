## 📊 Information Collection and Use

We collect several different types of information:

### Personal Data Directly Provided

When you create an account or use our Service, we may collect:

- **Email address** (for account creation and communication)
- **Name** (for profile and account management)
- **Password** (encrypted, never stored in plain text)
- **Profile information** (organization, role, preferences)
- **Billing information** (for paid plans)
- **Communication records** (support emails, chat logs)

### Automatically Collected Data

When you access our Service, we automatically collect:

- **IP address** (for security and analytics)
- **Device information** (browser, OS, device type)
- **Usage patterns** (which features you use, how often)
- **Access logs** (pages visited, time spent, actions taken)
- **Cookies** (for session management and preferences)

### Data from Third Parties

We may receive data from:

- **Payment processors** (Stripe) — for billing
- **Authentication providers** (Google, GitHub) — if using social login
- **Analytics providers** — aggregated usage data

## 🔄 How We Use Your Data

We use collected data for:

1. **Service provision** — To deliver features and functionality
2. **Account management** — Authenticating users, managing permissions
3. **Communication** — Sending updates, support responses, announcements
4. **Analytics** — Understanding usage patterns to improve the Service
5. **Security** — Detecting and preventing fraud, abuse, attacks
6. **Legal compliance** — Meeting regulatory requirements (GDPR, etc.)
7. **Customer support** — Resolving issues and improving assistance

**We do NOT:**
- Sell your personal data to third parties
- Use your data for unauthorized marketing
- Share your data with competitors
- Use personal data for AI training (unless explicitly agreed)

## 🔐 Data Security

We protect your data with:

- **Encryption at rest** (AES-256)
- **Encryption in transit** (TLS 1.2+)
- **Access controls** (role-based permissions)
- **Regular backups** (daily, tested restore)
- **Security audits** (quarterly)
- **Two-factor authentication** (2FA for admin accounts)

**Retention:** We retain personal data only as long as necessary. See Data Retention section.

If you are in the EU, you have rights under GDPR:

### Right to Access
You can request a copy of all data we hold about you.

### Right to Correction
You can request we correct inaccurate data.

### Right to Erasure
You can request we delete your personal data (subject to legal holds).

### Right to Restrict

## 🤖 AI & Data Processing

Synkra AIOX is an AI orchestration system. We process data as follows:

### Data Passed to AI Models
- **Always anonymized** — No personal identifiers in model inputs
- **Never for training** — Your data is not used to train our models
- **Short retention** — Deleted after processing (≤ 24 hours)
- **Encrypted in transit** — Protected with TLS 1.2+

### AI Processing Disclosure
When you use AI features (code generation, analysis, etc.), data is:
- Processed by our AI pipeline (Claude AI)
- Covered by Anthropic's privacy policy: https://www.anthropic.com/privacy
- NOT permanently stored or used for model training
- Deleted after processing window
