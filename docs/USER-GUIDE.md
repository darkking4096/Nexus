## Getting Started

### Prerequisites
- Instagram business account
- Email address
- Internet connection

### Step 1: Create Your Account

1. Visit **NEXUS** application
2. Click **"Sign Up"**
3. Enter your email and create a secure password (minimum 8 characters)
4. Click **"Create Account"**
5. You'll receive a confirmation email (check spam folder if needed)
6. Verify your email and log in

**Screenshot:** Account creation flow

---

### Step 2: Connect Your Instagram Profile

1. Log in to your NEXUS account
2. Click **"+ Connect Profile"**
3. Select **"Connect Instagram Business Account"**
4. You'll be redirected to Instagram's authorization page
5. Approve permissions for NEXUS to manage your content
6. Return to NEXUS and confirm the connection

**Permissions Requested:**
- Manage your content
- Access insights and analytics
- Schedule posts
- View competitor data

> **Note:** NEXUS securely stores your Instagram access tokens with AES-256 encryption. Your passwords are never shared or stored.

---

### Step 3: Configure Your Profile

1. After connecting, you'll see your profile details
2. Click **"Edit Profile Settings"**
3. Fill in your brand context:
   - **Voice:** Professional, Casual, Authoritative
   - **Tone:** Friendly, Educational, Inspiring
   - **Target Audience:** Who you're creating content for
   - **Objectives:** What you want to achieve (engagement, followers, sales)

4. Click **"Save Settings"**

**Example Configuration:**
```
Voice: Professional
Tone: Educational + Inspiring
Target Audience: B2B Marketing professionals and entrepreneurs
Objectives: Thought leadership, Build authority, Drive website traffic
```

## Profile Management

### View All Profiles

1. From the main dashboard, see all connected profiles
2. Each profile card shows:
   - Profile picture and username
   - Follower count
   - Current mode (Manual / Autopilot)
   - Last published date
   - Weekly engagement metrics

### Edit Profile Settings

1. Click on a profile card
2. Click **"Edit"** button
3. Update your preferences:
   - Brand voice and tone
   - Target audience focus
   - Content objectives
   - Autopilot configuration (if enabled)
4. Click **"Save"**

### Disconnect a Profile

1. Go to Profile Settings → **"Advanced"**
2. Click **"Disconnect Profile"**
3. Confirm the action

> **Warning:** This will remove all draft content for this profile. Published content remains on Instagram.

## Content Generation

### Generate a Single Post

#### Method 1: From Dashboard

1. Click **"+ New Content"**
2. Select target profile
3. Choose content type:
   - **Carousel** (multiple images with transitions)
   - **Reel** (short video)
   - **Static Image** (single image post)
   - **Story** (temporary content, 24h lifespan)

#### Method 2: From Profile

1. Open a specific profile
2. Click **"Generate Content"**
3. Proceed with content creation

---

### Generate Content with Research

For best results, NEXUS can analyze competitor content:

1. Select content type
2. (Optional) Enable **"Research Competitors"**
3. Enter competitor Instagram usernames to analyze their top posts
4. NEXUS will generate content inspired by successful strategies
5. Review and approve

**Example Research Data:**
```
Competitors to analyze: @competitor1, @competitor2
Trends to incorporate: #SustainableMarketing, #AITools
Best performing content types: Carousels, Educational threads
```

---

### Generated Content Preview

After generation, you'll see:

- **Caption:** Full post text with optimal hashtags
- **Hooks:** 3-5 opening lines to grab attention
- **Hashtags:** Researched and trending tags relevant to your niche
- **Engagement Estimate:** Predicted likes and comments
- **Visual Preview:** How it will appear on Instagram

### Edit Generated Content

1. Click **"Edit Caption"** to refine text
2. Click **"Edit Hashtags"** to customize tags
3. Reorder hooks or remove ones you don't like
4. Click **"Regenerate"** for new suggestions

## Publishing & Scheduling

### Publish Immediately

1. Review your content
2. Click **"Publish Now"**
3. Confirm on the final screen
4. Your post goes live on Instagram immediately

> **Note:** Instagram's cache may take 30 seconds to 2 minutes to reflect the new post.

---

### Schedule for Later

1. Review your content
2. Click **"Schedule"**
3. Select date and time
4. Confirm scheduling details
5. Post will be automatically published at the scheduled time

**Best Practices:**
- **Tuesday - Thursday:** Higher engagement rates
- **9:00 AM - 11:00 AM:** Peak audience activity
- **7:00 PM - 9:00 PM:** Evening engagement spike
- *Post times vary by industry and audience location*

---

### Manage Scheduled Content

1. Go to **"Scheduled Content"** from profile
2. View all upcoming posts with dates and times
3. Options:
   - **Reschedule:** Change publication date/time
   - **Edit:** Modify caption or hashtags
   - **Cancel:** Remove scheduled post

## Analytics & Metrics

### Dashboard Overview

Your main dashboard shows:

- **Total Followers:** Combined across all profiles
- **Weekly Engagement:** Likes + comments + saves (past 7 days)
- **Monthly Reach:** Unique accounts that saw your content
- **Content Performance:** Your best and worst performing posts

---

### Profile Analytics

1. Select a profile to see detailed metrics
2. View:
   - **Follower Growth:** Track new followers over time
   - **Engagement Rate:** How much audience interacts with your content
   - **Reach & Impressions:** How many see your content
   - **Best Content Types:** Which formats perform best
   - **Best Posting Times:** When your audience is most active

---

### Post-Level Analytics

1. Click on a published post
2. See real-time metrics:
   - **Engagement:** Likes, comments, saves, shares
   - **Reach:** How many accounts saw it
   - **Impressions:** Total times it was seen (includes repeats)
   - **Profile Visits:** How many visited your profile from this post
   - **Website Clicks:** (If link in bio) How many clicked through

---

### AI Recommendations

NEXUS analyzes your performance and suggests:

- **Optimal posting time:** When your audience is most active
- **Best content types:** Carousels vs. Reels vs. Static

- **Growth opportunities:** Untapped audience segments

## Autopilot Mode

### What Is Autopilot?

Autopilot automatically generates and schedules content based on your preferences. You approve content quality before it goes live.

---

### Enable Autopilot

1. Go to Profile Settings
2. Switch to **"Autopilot Mode"**
3. Configure:
   - **Publishing Schedule:** Days and times (e.g., M-W-F at 9 AM)
   - **Content Frequency:** How often to generate content
   - **Content Types:** Prefer carousels? Reels? Mix?
   - **Research:** Enable/disable competitor research

4. Click **"Enable Autopilot"**

**Example Configuration:**
```
Schedule: Monday, Wednesday, Friday
Time: 9:00 AM
Frequency: 3 posts per week
Content mix: 60% carousel, 30% reel, 10% static
Research: Analyze top 3 competitors weekly
```

---

### Approve/Reject Autopilot Content

1. You'll receive notifications when content is ready
2. Review the generated content in **"Pending Approval"** section
3. Options:
   - **Approve:** Confirms scheduling
   - **Regenerate:** Get new suggestions
   - **Manual Edit:** Fine-tune caption and hashtags
   - **Reject:** Discard and skip this post

---

### Pause or Disable Autopilot

1. Go to Profile Settings
2. Click **"Pause Autopilot"** (temporary) or **"Disable"** (permanent)
3. Any pending content can still be approved manually
4. Future autopilot posts won't be generated

## FAQ & Troubleshooting

### Q: How often can I generate content?

**A:** No limits! Generate as many posts as you want. You only publish when you approve.

---

### Q: Will my password be stored?

**A:** No. We use OAuth2 authentication with Instagram. Your password is never shared with NEXUS.

---

### Q: Can I edit content after publishing?

**A:** You can edit captions and hashtags directly on Instagram. NEXUS will show updated metrics after refreshing.

---

### Q: What if my scheduled post fails to publish?

**A:** Check our [Status Page](https://status.nexus.app). You'll receive an email notification and can retry manually.

---

### Q: How is my data secured?

**A:**
- All sensitive data (tokens) encrypted with AES-256-GCM
- HTTPS for all communications
- No password storage (OAuth2)
- Regular security audits

---

### Q: Can I use NEXUS for multiple accounts?

**A:** Yes! Connect as many Instagram profiles as you have. Manage all from one dashboard.

---

### Q: What if I don't like the generated content?

**A:** Click **"Regenerate"** for new suggestions, or manually edit any part of the caption and hashtags.

## Best Practices

✅ **Do:**
- Review content before publishing
- Post consistently (3-5 times per week optimal)
- Engage with comments within 2 hours of posting
- Test different posting times to find your peak
- Use trending hashtags in your niche
- Monitor competitor strategies

❌ **Don't:**
- Disable comments (reduces engagement)
- Post the same content multiple times
- Ignore analytics data
- Use unrelated hashtags (#tbt, #fff without context)
- Post at random times inconsistently
- Neglect community engagement
